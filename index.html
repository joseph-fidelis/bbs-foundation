Empowering Girls, One Voice at a Time

Every girl deserves safety, confidence, and a chance to thrive. At BBS Foundation, we're on a mission to make that possible.

Who We Are

BBS Foundation is a girl-focused nonprofit passionate about creating safe spaces, inspiring dreams, and equipping young girls with the tools to thrive. We advocate, educate, and empower, because we believe purpose starts young.

Our Vision

A world where every girl grows up safe, seen, and strong

Our Mission

To nurture and equip the girl child with the tools, confidence, and support she needs to live boldly, speak freely, and reach her full potential.

Our Core Values

Safety First: Protecting the physical, mental, and emotional well-being of every girl.
Boldness: Encouraging girls to own their voice and take up space.
Empowerment: Teaching practical life skills and self-leadership.
Community: Building sisterhood and supportive networks.

Our Work

What We Do

We focus on 3 core areas:

1

Safe Spaces & Mentorship:
We run programs that give girls room to speak, ask questions, and be mentored by trusted women.

2

Girl Child Advocacy & Campaigns:
Through school tours, social media, and grassroots campaigns, we challenge harmful norms and raise awareness on issues affecting girls.

3

Skills & Growth Workshops:
From confidence-building sessions to personal hygiene and goal-setting, we equip girls with real tools for real life.
